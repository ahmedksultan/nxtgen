Meet the Team

Think you would be a great addition to our team? We want to hear from you! As a quickly growing organization, we are always looking for college and high school students who share our values and mission. While we don't hire for specific positions, we want to know what you think you can bring to the table. Send us a short blurb (and your resume) at exec@projnextgen.org about who you are and the role you're hoping to play (and don't forget to include your favorite ice cream flavor!).
