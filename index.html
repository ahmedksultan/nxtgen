Who are we?

We want to make the government more accessible to students and increase youth involvement in the political process. Weâ€™re a non-partisan organization dedicated to the political and civic empowerment of the next generation.

Currently, we're working to create a network of Youth Policy Cabinets (YPCs) around the country in offices at all different levels of government. In our plan, YPCs will be cohorts of high school students that live or attend school in the office's district, and will meet on a regular schedule. During meetings, members of the cohorts will discuss policy issues important to their community, attend lectures with guest speakers and panelists and work toward developing solutions to current problems they find pressing.

Down the line, we hope to cultivate a network of YPCs that will allow for larger political discussions and region-wide events, as well as greater commuication and collaboration between students from different backgrounds and communities.
