Are you a student looking for a YPC in your community?

Don't see a YPC in your area?

Let's change that! Reach out to outreach@projnextgen.org with details about where you live and who your elected officals are, and we will get started on bringing a YPC to your community. Until then, don't hesitate reaching out to join our team.

Are you a government office seeking to start a YPC for youth in your community?

Having worked in both local and federal level governmental offices, we understand how busy offices and staffers are. We don't want to make this an added burden for you, so let us help make this easier for you. Establshing a YPC will be an active collaboration between the staff at the office and our team at Project NextGen. We'll help facilitate the recruitment process, plan basic training and sessions, as well as create materials, resources and meeting agendas. Your team would be responsible for hosting meetings and creating unique opportunities for students in these YPCs. Reach out to exec@projnextgen.org for our one pager and our curriculum 101 outline and together lets create an authentic youth-adult partnership, .

Are you a partner organization hoping to collaborate with us?

We are looking to collaborate with like-minded organizations that value student voice, youth empowerment, and civic engagement. Reach out to contact@projnextgen.org with how we can help you or how you'd like to help us.
