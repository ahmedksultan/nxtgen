Establshing a YPC will be an active collaboration between the staff at the office and our team at Project NextGen. We'd be happy to facilitate the recruitment process, plan basic training sessions and create materials, resources and meeting agendas. Your team would be responsible for hosting meetings and creating unique opportunities for students in these YPCs.

For more information, we encourage you to look at our one pager and curriculum outline, but please do keep in mind that we are flexible and open to creating YPCs under terms that work with your office's wants, needs, restrictions and guidelines.
